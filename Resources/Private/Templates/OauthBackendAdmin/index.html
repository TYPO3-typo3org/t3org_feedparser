Generate a Bearer Token for api.twitter.com

typo3.org needs a so-called "bearer token" from twitter in order to gain access to its API. You can generate it here.

First you need to register an application. The article on dev.twitter.com/docs/auth/tokens-devtwittercom describes how it is done. You can then enter in the Consumer Key and Secret here and you will get the bearer token.
